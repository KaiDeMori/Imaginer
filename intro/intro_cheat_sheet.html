Technical Details

Fonts

  • Andika (Font 1): A clear, sans-serif font designed for literacy.
  • Comic Neue (Font 2): A modern, more legible version of Comic Sans.
  • Noto Sans (Font 3): A clean, universal sans-serif font.
  • Orbitron (Font 4): The primary cinematic font. Used for the main title and "bloom" effects to give a sci-fi feel.
  • Quicksand (Font 5): A rounded sans-serif font with a friendly appearance.
