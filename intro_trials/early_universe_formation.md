## Visual & Animation Ideas

- **Layered AI-generated PNGs:** Use transparent PNGs of nebulae, galaxy streams, and cosmic clouds, generated by image AI, as layered backgrounds and foregrounds.
- **Animated Reveal:** Gradually fade in or slide layers to simulate the universe forming from diffuse light to structured galaxies.
- **Star Clusters:** Overlay twinkling star clusters, either as AI assets or generated with canvas/JS.
- **Cosmic Fog:** Use semi-transparent fog/cloud PNGs for depth, animating their opacity and blur.
- **Color Transitions:** Animate background gradients from warm to cool tones to represent cosmic evolution.
- **Subtle Camera Movement:** Apply slow zoom or pan effects for depth.
- **Performance:** Use canvas for many small elements; keep DOM light.

## AI-Generated Asset Integration

- Generate multiple PNGs with transparency for:
  - Nebulae (various shapes/colors)
  - Galaxy streams and filaments
  - Star clusters
  - Cosmic fog/cloud overlays
- Store assets in a dedicated folder (e.g., `assets/ai_universe/`).
- Use CSS/JS to animate and layer these assets dynamically.

## Implementation Notes

- No on-screen text for this step; focus on visuals.
